BCA Mobile



Overview


Project
Personal Project
Scope
UI Redesign
Tool
Adobe XD

BCA Mobile is a mobile banking application by Bank Central Asia (BCA). Despite being one of the biggest bank in Indonesia, their mobile UI/UX design is outdated and often frustrating. This is a personal project of mine and I am in no way affiliated with BCA


Research


Below are comments and complaints made by users from Twitter and the App Store.

Points taken from research:

  • Color gradations that are too much to give a feeling that is less comfortable in the eye, although it can make it easier for users to distinguish buttons and other components.
  • Usability is too complicated, app requires users to register the destination account number in a different menu before making a transaction.
  • Difficulty in accessing information regarding balance and mutation info.
